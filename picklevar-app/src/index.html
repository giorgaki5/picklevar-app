PickleVAR

Line Call Review System

Version 1.0.0

How to Use

Setup

1
Position your phone at floor level, pointed at the line you want to monitor.
2
Use the Crop feature to focus on just the line area for longer recording buffer.

During Play

The app continuously records. When there's a disputed call, tap "Review Last Ball" to instantly rewind.

Review Mode

1
Use the scrubber or ±15s buttons to find the moment.
2
Use Frame buttons to step through frame-by-frame.
3
Tap "Set Loop" to replay the moment continuously.
4
Adjust playback speed (0.1× to 1×) for slow motion.

Tips

• Use a small tripod or phone holder for stability
• Smaller crop region = longer buffer time
• 0.1× speed is best for close calls

Settings

Frame Rate (FPS)

Higher FPS = smoother slow-motion but shorter buffer time.

60 FPS - Smoothest slow-motion, shorter buffer

Camera

Camera selection is available on the live monitoring screen.

Current Settings

• Frame Rate: 60 FPS
• Buffer: Up to 2 minutes (with crop)
• Resolution: Auto-optimized
